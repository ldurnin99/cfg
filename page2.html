Who Are We?

Badlands Amateur Running Club was set up by three students as a way of motivating each other to stop sitting on their arses and get skinny. Based in the Badlands of St Andrews (the sports centre is a trek) we meet 3 times a week running a variety of routes and distances, perfect for those just starting on their running journey!
